from a cool desktop model to keep you going through a tough day to an
epic toy for younger siblings!

How is it done?

Well, when you make a resin model, you pour a mixture of resin and
hardener into a cup and stir carefully. Then, you can give it more style by
adding glitter, sequins and lots and lots of colour! Pour it into a special
mould and leave it to dry for about 24 hours. Then, you peel the mould
away and you have with a perfect model!
