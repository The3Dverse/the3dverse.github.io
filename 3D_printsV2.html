How does it all work?

Well, when I first discovered 3D printing, it seemed like magic so I'm
gonna try and simplify it as much as possible. *Inhale* Sadly, a 3D printer can't just produce things out of nowhere. We need to
load a reel of plastic into the printer.


This plastic is called filament and we use a motor to force this filament
through a tube and into a thing called a hotend.


The hotend heats it and pushes it out of a nozzle. When it comes out,
it cools and becomes hard. We move the hotend using motors while this
happens and make 1 layer. We stack a bunch of layers and get a 3D print!
